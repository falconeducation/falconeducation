Computer Vision Workshop Part - I

They are watching us!

Cameras are no longer just tools to take pictures. Smartphones are using cameras to scan QR codes, to create virtual Pok√©mon that interacts with the environment and much more!! Tesla uses sophisticated algorithms to automatically drive their cars. Hollywood uses complicated CGI effects to create awe-inspiring movies. Do you want to ride the AI wave? Computer Vision is just the thing for you. In this workshop students get to learn the basic image processing and deep-learning concepts required to perform various computer vision tasks.

Course Details

Date: December 5 - December 13
Duration: 4 Days (2 Weekends)
Mode of learning: Live online class
Pre-Requisites: Basic Programming knowledge (Python)


Course Contents

DAY 1

Introduction

- Basics of Python and Numpy
- Loading and Saving Images
- Loading and Saving Videos
- Pixels and Regions of Interest

DAY 2

Image Operations

- Colour systems and colour masks
- Arithmetic and Logical operations
- Mini Project 1:
     Invisibility Cloak

DAY 3

Image Transformations

- Geometrical transforms
- Morphological transforms
- Gradients and Edge detection
- Hough transform

DAY 4

Projects

- Image Contours
- Mini projects 2 & 3:
     Detecting Traffic Lanes
     Object Tracking With Contours
