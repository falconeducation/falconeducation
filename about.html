Education on cutting-edge topics like AI and Embedded Systems.


Falcon is an organization co-founded by alumni of Anna University, MIT Campus. Falcon strives to provide high-quality education on cutting-edge topics like artificial intelligence and embedded systems. We share the new India's aspirations of self-reliance and seek to produce the visionaries and the skilled workforce required to realise that objective, through workshops which are specially designed with our project-based curriculum.

What we do

Conducting many successful workshops with our team, comprising of people who have worked on a wide array of projects.


We have a history of conducting many successful workshops in inter-collegiate symposiums. Our team comprises of people who have worked on a wide array of projects such as Self Driving Cars, Surveillance UAVs, Home Automation, Analysis of Skin Burns, etc., demonstrating our profiency with the use of promising technologies like Artificial Intelligence and Robotics and now, we are thriving to do more.
