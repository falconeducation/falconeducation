Basics Of Python Course

The first step to glory!

Python is the fastest growing language right now. Python is omnipresent. Be it Machine Learning, Embedded Systems, Computer vision, Natural Language Processing, Back-end development, Internet of things or any kind of automation, Python is the go-to language for a quick and easy solution. In this Basics of Python Course, participants are taught how to use the important modules and features of Python. Participants also get to work on innovative puzzles and Physics problems such as a rocket launch simulation! Why wait when its for free!
